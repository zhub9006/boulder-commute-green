# Boulder Commute Tips

## Walking Tips
- Pearl Street Mall is the pedestrian-only heart of downtown
- Carry water - Boulder altitude (5,430 ft) dehydrates faster
- Dress in layers - mornings can be 10-15F cooler
- Use Boulder Creek Path for a more scenic route

## Biking Tips
- Bike lanes are well-marked on most of this route
- Canyon Boulevard has a protected bike path along the creek
- Paceline Bike Center (downtown) for tune-ups
- Boulder B-cycle bike-share stations near your route
- Register your bike with the City for free anti-theft engraving

## Year-Round Considerations
- Winter: Streets cleared promptly, watch for ice on bridges
- Summer: Carry water! Limited shade on parts of the route
- Spring/Fall: Prime biking seasons with mild temps
